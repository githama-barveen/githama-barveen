# 📊 Customer Goods Ad-Hoc Insights (SQL)
Project Overview:
Atliq Hardware is one of India's leading computer hardware manufacturers. Management requested ad-hoc analysis to support data-driven decisions. Ten business questions were solved using SQL to generate meaningful insights.

Tools Used: MYSQL, Excel

Insights: 
Atliq Exclusive operates in 8 APAC markets, with India leading. Unique products grew by 36.33% in 2021, especially in Accessories, while Networking had the lowest. AQ HOME Allin1 Gen 2 had the highest manufacturing cost and AQ Master Wired X1 MS the lowest. Flipkart got the highest discount. Sales dipped in Mar–Apr 2020, peaked in Nov 2020, with Q1 highest in quantity. Retailer channels led sales, and N&S was the top division.

# 📡 Telecom Customer Analysis (Power BI)
Project Overview:
AtliQo is one of the leading telecom providers in India and launched its 5G plans in May 2022 along with other telecom providers. However, AtliQo’s business director requested their analytics team to provide a comparison report of KPIs between pre and post-periods of the 5G launch.

Tools Used: Microsoft Power BI

Insights: 
Comparative Analysis of Pre and Post-5G Launch Performance: An evaluation of the key metrics by pre and post of the 5G launch, focusing on Revenue, ARPU, Monthly Active Users, and Unsubscribed Users.
Marketshare Level Performance: An analysis of Atliqo’s position among competitors in the market.
Plans Revenue Analysis: Evaluation of historical plans sales pattern between pre and post of the 5G launch
